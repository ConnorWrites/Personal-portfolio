About Conrad

Front End Developer / Author

I am a proudly South African software developer and author with an innate passion for animals, nature, and keeping the natural wonder of the world alive. I currently work in Pretoria, South Africa, where I live with my beautiful wife, Christine, and our two small children who are little bundles of never-ending energy. During the five years I spent working abroad as an English teacher I discovered a love for taking something good and making it great, and as excellence is something I pursue in every area of my own life I strive to keep supporting every one of my students, clients, and colleagues on their journey towards a higher standard. I recently obtained a certificate in legacy javaScript algorithms and data structures, and consistantly develop and improve on my existing set of skills.
